The story explores themes of friendship, courage, and the eternal conflict between good and evil. Beyond the books, the franchise expanded into films, captivating fans worldwide. With its engaging narrative and memorable characters, "Harry Potter" became a cultural phenomenon, leaving a lasting legacy in literature and pop culture.
